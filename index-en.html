About me

I’m a backend developer with experience building full administrative platforms, online reservation systems, payment order generation, receipt uploads, and process automation. I mainly work with Laravel and MySQL, and I also use React, Alpine.js, and Python for data science. I've developed real-world projects for companies like Merco (public site and internal admin system) and Alpek Adventures, a tourism agency based in Monterrey. For them, I built a CRM-style platform that allows clients to register, book tours, choose what to pay and to which bank account, generate their payment order, and upload proof of payment. The system helps administrators review, validate, and assign received payments, as well as generate legal documents and reports in Word and Excel. I’ve also worked with machine learning models applied to financial analysis, and developed mobile prototypes using Ionic and Flutter as part of my Software Technology Engineering degree at FIME, UANL. I specialize in building solutions that improve efficiency, reduce errors, and enable businesses to scale their digital operations in an organized and secure way.
